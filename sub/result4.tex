
\subsection{Maximum monthly mean values of chlorophyll-a concentration.}
 
Figure \ref{fig:monthly_max} shows the maximum values of monthly mean chlorophyll-a concentration in the East Sea. It shows that the LAC data have higher maximum value in the monthly mean chlorophyll-a concentration compared to the GAC data. The maximum value of the differences between the LAC and GAC data was 80 $\rm mg~m^{-3}$ on June, 2003. The maximum values of both the LAC and GAC data did not show a strong realtionship with the seasons.
